Our idea :

The idea is that using your app, you can grow trees to block your phone while the tree grows. This allows you to keep your focus and continue working peacefully.

How to choose?

The type of tree you choose allows you to block your phone for a longer or shorter period of time and earns you more premium. In addition, you have a greater environmental impact depending on the tree you choose and the soil you use. The objective is that you stay focused for a long time and prevents you from going on tiktok or instagram.
